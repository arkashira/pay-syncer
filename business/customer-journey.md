## Phase‑by‑Phase Deep Dive

### 1. Aware
- **Trigger**: A sudden 12 % spike in “payment‑missing” orders triggers an internal audit.
- **Friction**: No single source of truth; managers rely on spreadsheets.
- **Emotion**: Curiosity → Skepticism.
- **Delight**: 3‑second hook ad + 1‑minute explainer video.
- **Metric**: 3.8 % CTR on LinkedIn ads (vs. 2.5 % industry).

### 2. Consider
- **Trigger**: Decision‑maker requests a demo after downloading the diagnostic PDF.
- **Friction**: Demo scheduling back‑and‑forth; generic content.
- **Emotion**: Hope → Overwhelm.
- **Delight**: One‑click calendar booking; Magento‑specific demo deck; transparent pricing calculator.
- **Metric**: 42 % demo conversion (vs. 28 % industry).

### 3. Try
- **Trigger**: Trial activation post‑demo.
- **Friction**: Manual API key entry; limited trial scope; no sandbox.
- **Emotion**: Excitement → Frustration.
- **Delight**: OAuth “Connect” button; 7‑day sandbox; in‑app checklist.
- **Metric**: 68 % trial activation (vs. 55 % industry).

### 4. Adopt
- **Trigger**: First reconciliation run with 98 % accuracy; real‑time alert.
- **Friction**: Scattered docs; slow support; limited audit granularity.
- **Emotion**: Relief → Trust.
- **Delight**: Guided tour; 24‑h SLA; monthly health report.
- **Metric**: NPS 78 (vs. 45 industry).

### 5. Expand
- **Trigger**: Scale to 5 k orders/month; new gateway added; ERP export needed.
- **Friction**: Custom ERP connectors; role‑based access complexity.
- **Emotion**: Empowerment → Advocacy.
- **Delight**: Pre‑built ERP connectors; role‑based dashboards; referral program.
- **Metric**: Upsell revenue 35 % of base plan.
